# Vairavanpatti — Nagarathar temple-clan

One of the nine Nagarathar (Nattukottai Chettiar) temple-clans — the structural analogue of
a Kongu Vellala kootam, but defined by a clan-temple (Nava Kovil) rather than a totem.

This entry exists so the cross-community **compare** view can place a Nagarathar temple-clan
beside a Kongu Vellala kootam such as Kadai. It is deliberately kept **out** of the
145-kootam manifest, force graph, and lineage selector — those surfaces are specific to the
Kongu Vellala kootam system, and the Nagarathar are a different caste with a structurally
parallel but historically unrelated clan system.
