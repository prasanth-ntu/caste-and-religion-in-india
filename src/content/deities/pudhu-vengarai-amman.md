# Pudhu Vengarai Amman

Kuladeivam (clan deity) for [[senganni]] kootam lineages in the Kongu region. Temple is located in **Vengarai**, Namakkal district, Tamil Nadu.

The temple village **Vengarai** takes its name from the *vengai maram* (வேங்கை மரம், *Pterocarpus marsupium*, Indian Kino) — the totem tree of the Senganni kootam. The kootam-totem-village-deity chain is therefore tightly bound.
