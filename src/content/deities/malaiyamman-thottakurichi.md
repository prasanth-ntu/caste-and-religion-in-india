# Malaiyamman, Thottakurichi

Kuladeivam (clan deity) for **Porulthantha kootam** (ancient name *Porulaanthai*) lineages of the Kongu Vellala Gounder community. The shrine — the **Thottakurichi Malaiyamman Temple** — sits at **Punjai Thottakurichi**, a town panchayat in **Karur district**, Tamil Nadu, near Pugalur in the historical Kongu Nadu.

🟡 **Family-attested.** The Porulthantha ↔ Malaiyamman kuladeivam binding rests on family oral tradition; the temple location is confirmed from a Google Maps pin (Punjai Thottakurichi, Karur district).
