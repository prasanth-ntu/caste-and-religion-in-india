# Vairavar Swamy (Vairavanpatti)

Clan deity of the Vairavanpatti temple-cluster — one of the nine Nagarathar (Nattukottai Chettiar) clan-temples (Nava Kovil). The temple's presiding deity (moolavar) is Lord Valaroli Nathar, a form of Shiva, with consort Vadivudai Ammai; the *prominent* deity is Bhairava — known in Tamil as Vairavan / Vairavar, a fierce guardian form of Shiva whose vahana is the dog — and it is from Vairavar that the village of Vairavanpatti, in Sivaganga district near Karaikudi, takes its name. Clan and popular usage accordingly know it as the "Vairavar" temple. The present stone temple was reconstructed by the Nagarathar in 1864, and is also counted among the Ashta Bhairava sthalams.
