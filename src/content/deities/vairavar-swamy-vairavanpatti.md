# Vairavar Swamy (Vairavanpatti)

Clan deity of the Vairavanpatti temple-cluster — one of the nine Nagarathar (Nattukottai Chettiar) clan-temples (Nava Kovil). The deity is Kala Bhairava, known in Tamil as Vairavan / Vairavar, a fierce guardian form of Shiva whose vahana is the dog. The village of Vairavanpatti, in Sivaganga district near Karaikudi, takes its name from the deity. The present stone temple was reconstructed by the Nagarathar in 1864, and is also counted among the Ashta Bhairava sthalams.
