# Gounder (கவுண்டர்)

A title, not a caste. In the Kongu region, "Gounder" indexes Kongu Vellala Gounder identity specifically — the cultivator community organised into 145 kootams, each with a totem and exogamy rules. Elsewhere in Tamil Nadu the same title is used by different communities with different histories.
