# Vellala (வேளாளர்)

The broad Tamil cultivator caste-cluster. The Kongu Vellala Gounder community is one regional sub-group within this cluster — distinguished by the 145-kootam structure, the Gounder title, the Kongu landscape, and a distinct dialect of Tamil.
