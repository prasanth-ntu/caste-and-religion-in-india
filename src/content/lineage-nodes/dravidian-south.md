# Dravidian South India

The peninsular south. Carries the Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, Telugu, and Tulu linguistic spheres, the Sangam-era Tamilakam political geography, and the medieval imperial dynasties (Pallava, Chola, Pandya, Chera, Hoysala, Vijayanagara) that built much of the surviving stone temple architecture.
