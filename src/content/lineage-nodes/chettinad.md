# Chettinad (செட்டிநாடு)

The homeland of the Nattukottai Chettiar / Nagarathar — a cluster of villages in southern Tamil Nadu (Sivaganga and Pudukkottai districts), structured around nine ancestral temple-clans. A parallel worked example to the Kongu Nadu region: a different caste branch (Vaishya-classified mercantile rather than Shudra-classified cultivator), organised by exogamous temple-clusters rather than totem-kootams.
