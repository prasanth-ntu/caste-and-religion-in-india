# Kongu Nadu (கொங்கு நாடு)

The seven historical districts of the Kongu region in western Tamil Nadu. Known for tank-fed agriculture, the Vellala cultivator class, and a distinct dialect of Tamil. Sat at the meeting point of Chera, Chola, and Pandya spheres of influence and shifted hands repeatedly through the Sangam and medieval periods.
