# Varna System

Varna (வர்ணம்) is the theoretical schema. Jāti (ஜாதி / குலம்) is the lived endogamous group. Conflating the two is one of the most common analytical errors in popular caste discourse, and it is what this lineage tree is partly designed to disentangle.
