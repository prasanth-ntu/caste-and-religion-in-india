# Keluthi Kootam

Keluthi Kootam is one of the 145 kootams of the Kongu Vellala Gounder community. Its totem is **Catfish (Wallago attu, traditional Tamil name keluthi)** (கெளுத்தி).

Per standard kootam practice, members marry into other kootams; the listed exogamy partners reflect documented or commonly-cited maaman-machan alliances (sample edges for the V3 force graph — not exhaustive).
